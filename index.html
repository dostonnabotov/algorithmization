Temperatures

From Celcius to Fahrenheit

Output: 0 \(^\circ F\)

Formula

$$T_F=({9\over 5} * T_C) + 32$$

From Fahrenheit to Celcius

Output: 0 \(^\circ C\)

Formula

$$T_C={5\over 9} * (T_F - 32)$$

From Celcius to Kelvin

Output: 0 \(^\circ K\)

Formula

$$T_K=T_C+273.15$$

From Kelvin to Celcius

Output: 0 \(^\circ C\)

Formula

$$T_C=T_F-273.15$$

From Fahrenheit to Kelvin

Output: 0 \(^\circ K\)

Formula

$$T_K={5\over 9} * (T_F - 32)+273.15$$

From Kelvin to Fahrenheit

Output: 0 \(^\circ F\)

Formula

$$T_F={9\over 5} * (T_K - 273.15)+32$$
